Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese
  • Ground Meat
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Spices
  • Bechamel

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat
  2. Mix the sauce with the meat
  3. Prepare the bechamel
  4. Soften the lasagna sheets
  5. Layer the lasagna sheets with meat sauce and bechamel
  6. Cook and serve
